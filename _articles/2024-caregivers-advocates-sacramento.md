## Overview

This feature article covered the California Coalition on Family Caregiving's conference and advocacy day in Sacramento, where caregivers, advocates, and policymakers gathered to discuss the future of caregiving in California and advocate for caregiver-supportive legislation.

## Contribution

- Documented coalition's first major advocacy day event
- Highlighted personal stories of family caregivers and their policy advocacy
- Explained priority bills supporting caregivers (AB 518, AB 2428)
- Provided comprehensive event coverage including panel discussions and legislative meetings
- Connected caregiver experiences to policy solutions

## Process and methods

- Attended full-day conference and advocacy event in Sacramento
- Conducted interviews with caregivers, advocates, and coalition leaders
- Documented panel discussions and legislative briefings
- Synthesized information about priority legislation
- Collaborated with USC Leonard Davis School communications team
- Captured quotes and perspectives from multiple stakeholders

## Impact

The article provided visibility for the California Coalition on Family Caregiving's work and documented caregivers' advocacy efforts, serving as a record of the coalition's first year achievements and helping to raise awareness of caregiver-supportive legislation among USC community and broader audience.
