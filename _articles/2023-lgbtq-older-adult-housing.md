## Overview

This resource guide provided practical information and resources for LGBTQ older adults and their families seeking age-appropriate and affirming housing options, addressing unique challenges faced by this population.

## Contribution

- Compiled comprehensive housing resources for LGBTQ elders
- Addressed health equity issues in senior living
- Provided practical tips for evaluating housing options
- Highlighted LGBTQ-affirming senior communities
- Connected housing to health and wellbeing outcomes

## Process and methods

- Researched LGBTQ-affirming senior housing options
- Interviewed experts in LGBTQ aging and senior services
- Compiled national and regional resources
- Fact-checked with GoodRx medical review team
- Developed accessible, actionable content

## Impact

Published on GoodRx's health platform, reaching millions of readers and providing critical information to an underserved population navigating senior housing decisions.
